Meet the team!

Here are the members of Tornado Alley Paranormal.

Mistie Cole

Mistie is a the lead investigator and co-founder of Tornado Alley Paranormal.

Mistie has been experiancing paranormal activity her whole life, so about fifteen years ago she decided to join her first paranormal team and after being apart of several different teams , she has decided that it was time to start her own. She has done several different T.V. shows including Tornado Alley's episode called Twisted Believers for the weather channel and My Ghost Story Caught on Camera for the Biography Channel. Helping people who have experianced the unexplained and the love for historical sites are the main reasons in which she is so dedicated to the paranormal field.

Harry Wheeler

Harry is an equipment technician and investigator.

I was born in Marietta Ohio. In 1967 I moved to Anchorage Alaska and remained there until 1994 when I moved to Grove Oklahoma.

When at my grandparents house in Ohio I walked by the bathroom and there was a man sitting there. I went to tell my grandparents and they came upstairs only to find no one was there. I believe that was the first apparition I had seen. When I was with another paranormal team I saw a shadow person at a historical home we were investigating. I am now excited to be a member with Tornado Alley Paranormal.

Doris Berlin

Doris is an investigator and reaseacher.

I was born in Broken Arrow Ok. and grew up in Fairbanks Alaska. The first paranormal experiance that I had was in our house when I was a teenager. I woke up in the middle of the night with a very dark figure of a man standing in the doorway of my bedroom. I imediately bolted to that door only for it to disappear. The next incident was at the same house but out in the backyard. I was playing with our dog and felt a firm hand on my shoulder. I know there was no body there. I know there were other instances but I just shrugged them off like most pther people would. Couple year ago I went with a friend to a ghost tour and history tour that eventually led to me becoming a paranormal investigator with that team. We encountered paranormal activity in several of the houses and businesses in the Carthage area. Due to peronal choices I chose to leave this team and join with Tornado Alley Paranormal.
